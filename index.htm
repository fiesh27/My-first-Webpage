A Quick Rundown

Hello! My name is Johnathan Hicks. I am 29 years old, married now for almost 6 years, and have a beautiful baby girl. I currently live in Strasburg, CO, where I have lived my entire life. As a matter of fact, I am the 4th generation to grow up and live here!

Strasburg is located 30 miles East of Denver. My little town only measures about 1 square mile with nothing but fields in all directions. We don't even have a stop light! I love it here because of the open spaces. I have always been a natural introvert, so big cities don't really agree with me.

I have worked at John's Repair as an auto mechanic for most of my life. After working for the previous owner, my father bought the business 25 years ago. Naturally, I grew up doing what he does. I have had other jobs here and there, but always ended up coming back to the automotive industry. I believe my lack of education in other areas is to blame. Right now, I make a mediocre income working for my father. Not that he hasn't tried to help, but in a small town, there is only so much business. I have friends that work in the Software field, and decided to reach out to them to find out more. After talking to them, I decided that coding is something that I can be good at and enjoy; something that doesn't tear my body up physically anymore; something that I know can help me provide for my family in the way that I know they deserve.

So, in conclusion, I know that this will a good avenue for me. I know that it is something that I can do if I put my mind to it, and I KNOW that it is something that can potentially change my life forever!
